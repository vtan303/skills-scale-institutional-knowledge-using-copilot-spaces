# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and foster team effectiveness. They coach the team on Scrum practices and help optimize delivery velocity while maintaining a healthy team dynamic.

### Responsibilities
- Organize and facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and reviews
- Remove blockers and impediments that hinder team progress
- Coach team members on Scrum/Agile practices and continuous improvement
- Shield the team from external distractions during sprints
- Help the team establish sustainable pace and sustainable practices
- Identify process improvements and facilitate team adoption

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and predictability
- Create psychological safety and continuous learning culture
- Reduce time spent on impediments and distractions
- Improve team collaboration and morale

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works closely with Project Manager** to ensure delivery alignment and escalation protocol
- **Partners with Developers** to remove technical blockers and optimize workflow
- **Collaborates with Product Manager** to clarify priorities and manage scope
- **Supports all team members** in understanding and following agreed processes

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-ones with team members when coaching or unblocking
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers advocate for user experience, create design artifacts, and ensure usability and accessibility across all deliverables. They translate user needs into intuitive, delightful solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and translate insights into design requirements
- Develop user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Collaborate with Product Manager on product vision and usability goals
- Provide design feedback and quality assurance during code reviews
- Document design decisions and maintain design systems
- Test designs with users and iterate based on feedback

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered solutions that exceed expectations
- Maintain consistency across products and experiences
- Reduce support burden through intuitive design
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design principles

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Interfaces between Product Manager and Developers** to translate requirements into design specifications
- **Collaborates with Developers** on technical feasibility and implementation details
- **Partners with QA/Testing** to ensure design acceptance criteria are clear
- **Reports to Project Manager** on design timelines and dependencies

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design syncs with Product Manager
- Design reviews with cross-functional stakeholders
- PR descriptions and design system documentation

---

## Solutions Architect

### Role Summary
Solutions Architects guide overall technical architecture and strategic integration decisions. They ensure that systems are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term organizational goals.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate technical vision and architectural standards
- Conduct design reviews and evaluate technical approaches for major features
- Ensure system integration, scalability, and performance requirements are met
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and dependencies
- Mentor Developers on architectural best practices
- Evaluate and recommend technology choices and third-party integrations

### Goals
- Build systems that scale with business needs
- Reduce technical debt and maintain long-term code health
- Enable faster feature delivery through well-designed architecture
- Ensure compliance and security in system design

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Partners with Developers** to review code and architecture decisions
- **Works with Project Manager** to identify technical dependencies and risks
- **Collaborates with Product Manager** to understand future product direction
- **Engages with QA/Testing** on performance and reliability requirements

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Risk register updates for technical concerns
- Mentoring sessions and technical documentation

---

## QA Lead / Testing Specialist

### Role Summary
QA Leads ensure quality standards and testing processes are upheld throughout the project lifecycle. They own the quality strategy, test plan, and acceptance validation.

### Responsibilities
- Establish test strategy, test plans, and quality acceptance criteria
- Design and execute functional, performance, and security tests
- Coordinate test execution and manage bug triage and prioritization
- Report on quality metrics, coverage, and readiness for release
- Work with Developers to improve testability and implement automated tests
- Validate compliance and security requirements

### Goals
- Maintain high quality standards and user satisfaction
- Catch defects early and reduce production incidents
- Provide confidence in release readiness
- Improve team testing practices and automation

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Collaborates with Developers** to review acceptance criteria and improve test coverage
- **Works with Project Manager** on release readiness and risk communication
- **Partners with Product Manager** to validate feature acceptance
- **Supports QA team** in test execution and defect management

### Typical Communication
- Quality review meetings and test status reports
- Release readiness checks and metrics
- Bug tracking and prioritization discussions

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts translate business needs into clear, actionable requirements and acceptance criteria. They bridge the gap between business stakeholders and delivery teams.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document requirements from business stakeholders and users
- Draft user stories, acceptance criteria, and detailed specifications
- Validate proposed solutions against business goals and constraints
- Facilitate requirements review and clarification with stakeholders
- Analyze impact of changes and identify dependencies
- Document business rules and process flows

### Goals
- Ensure traceability from business need to delivered solution
- Reduce rework due to unclear or incomplete requirements
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and adoption
- Enable faster delivery through clear specifications

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works closely with Product Manager** to define and refine product requirements
- **Collaborates with Project Manager** on scope, timeline, and change management
- **Partners with Developers** to clarify technical feasibility and design implications
- **Engages with QA/Testing** to define acceptance criteria and test scenarios

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Specification documents and user story backlog
- Change impact analysis and traceability matrices
