# OctoAcme — Project Planning

## Purpose
Turn an approved initiative into an actionable plan and backlog for delivery.

## Objectives
- Break work into shippable increments
- Identify dependencies and risks
- Align timelines, releases, and responsibilities

## Activities
1. Kickoff meeting with stakeholders and delivery team
2. Create prioritized backlog with acceptance criteria
3. Estimate scope (T-shirt sizing or story points)
4. Define Definition of Done (DoD)
5. Identify dependencies and integration points
6. Create release plan and milestone map

## Role Responsibilities During Planning

### Project Manager
- Schedule and facilitate the planning kickoff
- Maintain the master timeline and milestone dependencies
- Coordinate resource availability and capacity planning
- Track external dependencies and escalate blockers

### Product Manager
- Present prioritized features and rationale
- Define acceptance criteria and success metrics for each backlog item
- Answer clarifying questions on product vision and scope

### Developers
- Estimate work effort and identify technical risks
- Propose technical approach and identify integration needs
- Highlight assumptions and dependencies

### UX Designer
- Provide design mockups and interaction patterns
- Define UX acceptance criteria and usability requirements
- Identify design-related dependencies or sequencing needs

### QA Lead
- Define test approach and test coverage expectations
- Outline acceptance testing criteria aligned with business requirements
- Estimate QA effort and identify testing dependencies

### Scrum Master / Agile Coach
- Facilitate planning sessions and manage time
- Help team follow agreed planning process
- Identify process improvements from planning feedback

### Solutions Architect
- Review technical approach for scalability and integration
- Flag architectural risks or constraints
- Provide guidance on technology choices and integrations

### Business Analyst
- Clarify business requirements and constraints
- Document acceptance criteria and business rules
- Validate alignment between business needs and proposed solutions

## Sprint / Iteration Planning
- Timebox planning to agreed sprint length
- Pull items that meet DoD and have clear acceptance criteria
- Ensure team capacity is respected
- Assign clear ownership for each story

## Risk & Dependency Management
- Capture in Risk Register:
  - ID, Description, Impact, Probability, Owner, Mitigation
- Mark cross-team dependencies in the project board and escalate during weekly syncs
- Identify role-specific risks (e.g., design dependencies, QA capacity, architectural decisions)

## Planning Checklist
- [ ] Project kickoff held with all key roles represented
- [ ] Backlog prioritized and estimated by the delivery team
- [ ] Release timeline and milestones agreed with all stakeholders
- [ ] Definition of Done documented and agreed
- [ ] Initial test plan / QA approach drafted by QA Lead
- [ ] Design artifacts and UX requirements documented
- [ ] Technical risks and architectural decisions captured
- [ ] Business requirements validated and accepted
- [ ] Role owners assigned for each major initiative or dependency
